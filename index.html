Le Fermier - Game Jam 2026

How to Play

  • WASD / ZQSD / Arrow Keys to move
  • Green items are safe — collect them for points!
  • Items flash yellow before mutating...
  • Red items are hostile — they chase you!
  • You have 5 lives — lose them all, it's Game Over
  • Every 30s a new WAVE starts — faster and deadlier
  • Random "NOTHING IS SAFE" events turn ALL items hostile
  • Survive as long as you can and beat your high score!
